Marie Curie

The pioneer of radioactivity

Marie Curie's Timeline

  • 1867 - Born in Warsaw, Poland.
  • 1891 - Moves to Paris to study physics and mathematics.
  • 1895 - Marries Pierre Curie and begins collaboration.
  • 1898 - Discovers polonium and radium.
  • 1903 - Wins Nobel Prize in Physics.
  • 1911 - Wins Nobel Prize in Chemistry.
  • 1914 - Establishes Radium Institute.
  • 1934 - Dies of aplastic anemia.

"I am among those who think that science has great beauty."
